# Finvise — Founders and Team

## Co-Founders

### Onur Demirci — Original Founder & CEO (DEPARTED May 2025)
- **Background:** Senior Lecturer at University of Westminster, Westminster Business School
- **Education:** PhD in Mathematics Education; Cambridge affiliated (Homerton College, Faculty of Education)
- **Academic Role:** Programme Director for Foundation pathways at Westminster Business School
- **Fellowships:** FHEA (Higher Education Academy), FRSS (Royal Statistical Society), FRSA (Royal Society of Arts), FRGS (Royal Geographical Society)
- **Other:** Holds Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) from Department for Education
- **Status at Finvise:** Directorship terminated 21 May 2025. Ceased as PSC 1 July 2025.
- **Analysis:** Impressive academic credentials in *teaching* mathematics — not in applying it to property valuation. Fellowships are largely honorary/membership-based. An educator who founded a tech startup with no domain expertise in property.

### Max Boehnke — Co-Founder, now CEO (replaced Onur)
- **Background:** Former Chief of Staff and Portfolio Manager/Trader at Vanguard (active credit funds); then at Moonfare (private markets fintech) building Global Implementation Team
- **Education:** Regent's University London and Bayes Business School (formerly Cass)
- **Expertise:** Financial/investment management, fund trading, operational execution
- **Analysis:** Most commercially experienced founder. Real credentials in finance (Vanguard, Moonfare). But expertise is fund management and trading — zero property valuation background. Now running a property valuation company.

### Dr Shuwei Liu — Co-Founder & CTO
- **Background:** Former researcher at Max Planck Institute for the Physics of Complex Systems
- **Education:** PhD from Imperial College London (machine learning + quantum physics)
- **Awards:** Gold Medal Singapore Physics Olympiad, Silver Medal Singapore Maths Olympiad
- **Memberships:** Institute of Physics (IOP), International Max Planck Research Schools (IMPRS)
- **Analysis:** Genuinely brilliant technologist. Builds valuation models from a physics/data science perspective, not a property market perspective. May build mathematically elegant AVMs that miss real-world property valuation nuance.

### Hayley Lemm — Co-Founder & CPO
- **Background:** UX Designer; previously at University of Westminster (international site redesign)
- **Education:** University of Westminster (connection to Onur — likely how they met)
- **Notable:** Selected for Mayor of London's Digital Programme with D&AD and General Assembly
- **Expertise:** Product design, UX, digital content production
- **Analysis:** Junior-career designer elevated to co-founder/CPO. No property, finance, or valuation background.
