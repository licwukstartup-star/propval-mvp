# PropVal vs Finvise — Competitive Comparison

## Philosophy

| Dimension | Finvise | PropVal |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Core belief | Valuation is a computation problem | Valuation is a judgement problem with computational inputs |
| Role of AI | AI generates the value, valuer signs | AI provides tools and data, valuer decides |
| Role of valuer | Rubber stamp / regulatory credential | Decision-maker with AI-powered tools |
| RICS compliance | Badge on website, consultant holds it | Built into the workflow, valuer controls the process |

## Team & Execution

| Dimension | Finvise | PropVal |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Domain knowledge | None in founding team | Founder is MRICS, first valuer Finvise ever hired |
| Decision speed | 4 co-founders + meetings + politics | One brain, one vision |
| Build speed | Outsourced contractor — expensive, slow | AI-assisted, direct — near-zero cost |
| Cost per iteration | High (contractor rates + office + staff) | Near zero |
| Translation loss | Massive (4+ handoffs per feature) | Zero — founder is the domain expert AND builder |
| Vision coherence | Diluted by committee, original founder departed | Intact, single founder |

## Product

| Dimension | Finvise | PropVal |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| AVM approach | ANN-based (unvalidated methodology) | SEMV (LR + CNN + Stochastic Ensemble), validated per borough |
| Accuracy target | ±10% in 95% of cases, 85%+ overall | Tighter ranges, validated against known properties |
| Report generation | "2 seconds" — no human judgement possible | Valuer-controlled with AI assistance (Vibe Valuation) |
| Template system | Unknown | ARTG — 3 modes (ready-made/AI-upload/DIY) |
| Data architecture | Unknown, likely centralised | Borough-separated, UPRN spine, PostGIS, Supabase |

## Business Model

| Dimension | Finvise | PropVal |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Funding | $1M seed (Feb 2025), VC-dependent | Self-funded, conservative spend |
| Burn rate | High (office, staff, outsourced dev) | Near zero |
| Scaling approach | Hire more valuers, raise more money | Build platform for 1000+ valuers, scale through software |
| Revenue model | B2B to lenders (contact for pricing) | £35/seat/month + £35/full case + £5/research case |
| Control | UAE entity (Stellared Fzco) holds PSC | 100% founder-owned |

## Strategic Advantages — PropVal

1. **Founder IS the end user** — understands the profession from inside
2. **Saw Finvise fail firsthand** — every design decision informed by those mistakes
3. **Valuer-centric architecture** — not an afterthought, it's the foundation
4. **No VC dependency** — can't be pushed off vision by investors
5. **AI-assisted development** — builds faster and cheaper than Finvise's outsourced model
6. **SEMV > ANN** — validated model architecture, ANN proven to fail (+27.9% error)
7. **Borough-separated data** — respects market microstructure that a national AVM misses
8. **Full product vision** — not just AVM, but report lifecycle, citation graph, research platform

## Key Insight

Finvise proves the market exists — lenders want faster, AI-assisted valuations. But they've built the wrong product for the wrong reasons. PropVal is the correction: same market need, right architecture, right expertise, right economics.
