## What They Do
Finvise is an AI-powered property valuation platform targeting UK lenders (bridging, mortgage, HMO). They claim to be "the UK's first AI-powered valuation company regulated by RICS."

## Products
1. **Red Book Reports** — RICS-regulated desktop valuations, signed by human valuers, backed by £1M PI insurance
2. **AVM Reports** — Automated valuation model outputs with comparable properties and "explainable AI reasoning"

## Key Claims
- AVM accuracy within ±10% of human RICS valuation in 95% of cases
- Trained on 54+ million property and market data points
- Returns valuations in 92% of cases tested
- Validated against 3,000+ properties
- Reports delivered in "2 seconds"
- 85%+ accuracy rate (third-party listing)
- ISO 27001 and ISO 42001 certified
- London Stock Exchange marketplace member
- 11-50 employees (claimed)
