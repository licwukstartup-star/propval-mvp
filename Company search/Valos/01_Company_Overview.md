## What They Do
Valos is a report automation platform for commercial and residential property valuers. It automates data gathering, report compilation, and templating — reducing manual report-writing time. Importantly, Valos is **not an AVM** and does **not generate valuations**. It is a productivity tool that sits around the valuer's professional judgement, not a replacement for it.

Their tagline positioning: "Built by valuers, for valuers."

## Products
1. **Report Automation Platform** — Cloud-based tool that automates data gathering from 1,000+ data points (Ordnance Survey, Royal Mail, Environment Agency, Creditsafe, VOA, English Heritage) and compiles valuation reports using NLP/NLG technology
2. **Word Integration** — Outputs into Microsoft Word templates, fitting into existing surveyor workflows
3. **Data Aggregation** — Pulls environmental, planning, tenure, comparable, and location data into structured reports
