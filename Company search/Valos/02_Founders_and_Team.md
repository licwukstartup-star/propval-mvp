# Valos — Founders and Team

## Co-Founders

### Alex Kountourides MRICS — Co-Founder & CEO
- **Born:** May 1994 (age ~31)
- **Director since:** 14 May 2020 (founding day)
- **Education:** Mill Hill School; Birmingham City University
- **Previous employer:** Montagu Evans (Surveyor)
- **Qualified MRICS:** 2018
- **RICS Registered Valuer:** Yes
- **PSC:** 25-50% shares, 25-50% voting rights
- **Analysis:** Young surveyor who qualified at ~24. Genuine valuation experience at Montagu Evans (a respected London practice). Understands the pain points of manual report writing from firsthand experience. Has written LinkedIn articles on valuation topics (e.g. "The Fallacy of Off-Market Transactions"). The domain expert in the founding team.

### Robert (Rob) Davis MRICS — Co-Founder & COO
- **Born:** December 1993 (age ~32)
- **Director since:** 14 May 2020 (founding day)
- **Education:** University of Birmingham
- **Previous employer:** Workman LLP (Senior Surveyor)
- **Qualified MRICS:** November 2018
- **PSC:** 25-50% shares, 25-50% voting rights
- **Analysis:** Similar profile to Kountourides — young surveyor who qualified the same year. Workman LLP is a respected property management/surveying firm. Transitioned from asset management to operations at Valos. Second domain expert, covering the operational side.

### Jan Dexter — Co-Founder & CTO
- **Born:** October 1984 (age ~41)
- **Director since:** 14 May 2020 (founding day)
- **Previous employers:** UBS, Deutsche Bank, Kapital AI
- **NOT a PSC** — holds under 25% of shares
- **MRICS:** No — pure technology background
- **Analysis:** The most experienced team member by age and career. ~10 years building technology for top-tier investment banks. Serious engineering credentials. Recruited by the two surveyors specifically to build the platform. No property valuation background, but that's appropriate for a CTO role — the domain expertise sits with the two surveyor co-founders.

### Origin Story
Two young MRICS surveyors (Kountourides and Davis), both qualifying in 2018, became frustrated with the manual, repetitive aspects of valuation report writing. They recruited an experienced fintech developer (Dexter) and started building Valos in a garden shed. Company incorporated May 2020, first product launched July 2021.

**Two of three co-founders are MRICS Registered Valuers with genuine practice experience.** This is the inverse of Finvise, where zero co-founders had property valuation credentials.
