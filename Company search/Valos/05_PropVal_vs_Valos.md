# PropVal vs Valos — Competitive Comparison

## Philosophy

| Dimension | Valos | PropVal |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Core belief | Valuation reports take too long to write | Valuation needs better intelligence AND better reports |
| Role of AI | Automates data gathering and report compilation | Provides valuation intelligence, market signals, and report lifecycle |
| Role of valuer | Decision-maker (unchanged) — Valos just types faster | Decision-maker with AI-powered analytical tools |
| What it solves | Speed of report output | Quality of valuation input + speed of output |

## Team & Execution

| Dimension | Valos | PropVal |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Domain knowledge | 2 of 3 founders MRICS (Montagu Evans, Workman LLP) | Founder is MRICS, first valuer Finvise ever hired |
| Team size | ~10-15 employees | Solo founder, AI-assisted |
| Build approach | In-house CTO (UBS/Deutsche Bank) + team | AI-assisted development, near-zero cost |
| Funding | £500k (Dec 2021) | Self-funded |
| Decision speed | 3 co-founders, smaller committee than Finvise | One brain, one vision |
| Time in market | Since Jul 2021 (product launch) | Earlier stage |
| Enterprise clients | Knight Frank, Colliers, LSH, Eddisons | Not yet — building platform |

## Product Comparison

| Dimension | Valos | PropVal |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| AVM / price prediction | None — not their product | SEMV (LR + CNN + Stochastic Ensemble), borough-separated |
| Comparable analysis | Data aggregation only | Citation Graph — trackable, auditable comparable evidence |
| Report generation | NLP/NLG automation, Word output | ARTG — 3 modes (ready-made/AI-upload/DIY), Vibe Valuation |
| Data architecture | Per-report queries, no persistent spine | UPRN spine, PostGIS, property intelligence library |
| Market intelligence | None | GRAVITY (borough ranking), DARWIN (timing), smart money signals |
| Data sources | 1,000+ data points (OS, EA, VOA, etc.) | 19+ free UK government APIs, growing |
| Template system | Word templates with NLG fill | ARTG hybrid template engine |
| Report lifecycle | Generate and deliver | Copy pool → AI QA → review/approval → dual-signatory → client portal |

## Business Model

| Dimension | Valos | PropVal |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Target market | Commercial valuation firms (Knight Frank, Colliers) | Residential valuation surveyors |
| Revenue model | Enterprise SaaS (pricing not public) | £35/seat/month + £35/full case + £5/research case |

## Where Valos Wins

1. **Time in market** — product live since Jul 2021, 3+ years of iteration and client feedback
2. **Enterprise logos** — Knight Frank, Colliers, LSH are powerful social proof
3. **Proven PMF** — firms are paying for report automation, the problem is validated
4. **Clean execution** — no founder departures, no drama, steady growth
5. **Practitioner-founded** — "built by valuers for valuers" is genuine, not a marketing claim
6. **RICS Technology Partner** — formal RICS relationship, conference sponsorship

## Where PropVal Wins

1. **Valuation intelligence** — PropVal provides price prediction (SEMV), market signals, and comparable analysis that Valos doesn't attempt
2. **Data spine** — UPRN-anchored persistent intelligence library vs. per-report data queries
3. **Network effects** — every case enriches the platform; Valos has no equivalent
4. **Report lifecycle** — full workflow from creation to QA to approval to delivery; Valos only covers creation
5. **Residential focus** — Valos skews commercial; PropVal targets the higher-volume residential market
6. **Transparent pricing** — clear per-case model vs. opaque enterprise pricing
7. **Zero translation loss** — founder is the domain expert AND the builder
8. **Full product vision** — AVM + reports + citation graph + research platform + smart money signals

## Strategic Relationship

**Valos and PropVal are complementary more than competitive.** They operate at different layers:

- **Valos = report automation layer** — makes the existing valuation process faster
- **PropVal = valuation intelligence layer** — makes the valuation process smarter AND provides reports

If PropVal executes on ARTG (report generation), it directly competes with Valos on output speed. But PropVal's differentiation is upstream — the intelligence that informs the valuation, not just the document that records it.

The real competitive risk from Valos is **mindshare**. They have the enterprise logos. When a firm thinks "valuation tech," Valos comes to mind. PropVal needs to position as the next generation — not just faster reports, but better valuations.

## Key Insight

Valos proves that surveyors will pay for technology that saves them time on reports. But saving time is table stakes. The firms paying Valos for report automation will eventually want valuation intelligence too — comparable analysis, market signals, price prediction. Valos doesn't offer that. PropVal does. The question is whether PropVal can get to market before Valos expands upstream, or before a well-funded competitor builds both layers.
