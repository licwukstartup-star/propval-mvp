# AllRange — Brand Identity

## Brand Hierarchy

```
Anthropic          →  Claude           →  Opus
AllRange           →  NEON             →  SEMV
(company)             (product)           (model)
```

---

## AllRange — The Company

**All Range** — full coverage, every property, every angle, every value.

- Hidden wordplay: **AllRange ≈ "orange"** — the brand colour
- Role: the umbrella brand, the trust, the name on the report cover
- Scope: broad enough to house future products beyond SEMV
- Website: allrange.co.uk

---

## NEON — The Product

**N**o **E**xtra **O**nerous **N**otation — kill the paperwork, kill the donkey work.

- Hidden meaning: **neon lights** — cyberpunk aesthetic, electric, orange glow
- Role: the product clients see and use — *"Run it through NEON"*
- What it does: AI-powered property valuation intelligence
- This is what surveyors interact with daily

---

## SEMV — The Model

**S**tochastic **E**nsemble **M**arket **V**aluation — the engine under the hood.

- S — Stochastic (Monte Carlo randomness — 50,000 simulations)
- E — Ensemble (LR + CNN weighted blend)
- M — Market (market value output)
- V — Valuation (distribution + confidence intervals)
- Role: the technical/academic name — *"Powered by SEMV"*
- Engineers and academics know SEMV. Clients know NEON.
